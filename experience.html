Senior Capstone Project

In my senior year at Seattle University, I worked on a student team with SDI Engineering to implement an authorization and authentication system as well as telemetry system into their landing gear simulation software, GearSim. We used Oauth and Microsoft Azure technologies to integrate the systems into their MATLAB and Simulink based software. I loved focusing on the needs of the clients and creating something they were satisfied with.

Video Game Project

With two other students I created the prototype of a 2D platformer game using OpenGL and C++. I mainly focused on implementing visuals, story, and sound while the other students worked mainly on physics and controls. The game followed two playable sibling characters in an apocalyptic ghostly world as they tried to get back home to their village. It was super fun to work on and I hope I can do more projects like this in the future!
