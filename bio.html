Bio

Margery Carlos:

Developer,

Programmer,

Creative

Hi, I’m Margery. I’m a software engineer with the passion for both front-end and full stack development. I recently graduated from Seattle University with a bachelor's in computer science and a minor in creative writing. I’m passionate about actualizing creative visions, whether they are a client’s or my own. I also love solving unique problems in ways that put the user’s needs and desires first. Most days I work on finishing my nanodegree in artificial intelligence. For my most recent project, I built an adversarial game-playing agent for the game Isolation and wrote my own heuristic to improve the accuracy of the agent. I also try to find time to upload changes to my websites. Sometimes I’ll practice coding problems on HackerRank or on my own. In my free time, I dabble in a little bit of everything. Sometimes I’m chasing the best drama or trash comedy show on Netflix. Sometimes I’m pouring acrylic paint onto thick sheets of Bristol board and swirling the paint around into unique patterns. More recently I’ve been playing through old Xbox and Wii games I never got to play when I was young and finishing old Gameboy and DS Pokémon games I never beat. On weekends, I play online board games with one group of friends and play D&D with another. Late at night you’ll often find me on my phone, writing my next unpublished romance fantasy novel that may never see the light of day. This page is still in progress! Check back later for updates.
